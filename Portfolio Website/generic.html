Hi, I'm Aaryamaan Rao, a data enthusiast currently pursuing my Master's degree in Computer Science at Cornell University. My passion for data engineering and analytics has driven me to explore various technologies and tools such as Python, SQL, AWS, and Tableau. I am an AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner and a Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate holder. As an aspiring data engineer, I enjoy working on ETL design, dimensional modeling, and data visualization. My academic projects, including a cloud-based BI Tool for CU IT's Data Warehousing team and a Business Intelligence Project for Cornell University's Animal Hospital, have honed my skills and expanded my knowledge base in the field.

Apart from my academic pursuits, I have diverse interests and hobbies. I am an avid gamer and a Pokemon fan. In my free time, I love binge-watching fantasy shows, movies, and anime. I am also an animal lover and hope to foster and adopt a dog or cat in the future. I enjoy spicy food, and on rainy days, there's nothing more comforting than sipping on a cup of hot tea while listening to the pitter-patter of raindrops.
